Who Should I Talk To

Anyone who runs a business and accepts payments is a potential conversation, from the coffee shop on the corner to the salon down the street. Start in your spheres of influence, then expand into businesses you do not have a personal connection to yet.

What Strategy Should You Use

Lead with curiosity, not a script. Ask about their business first, listen for pain points around fees, service, or equipment, then share how you can help. Warm relationships open doors faster than a cold walk-in.

What Does Success Look Like

Success is not closing a deal on the spot, it is scheduling a follow up meeting. A full calendar of scheduled conversations is a stronger sign of momentum than any single sale.

What are the best ways to start the conversation?

Tap to flip
Best Ways to Start the Conversation

Introduce yourself and your connection to the community, ask a genuine question about their business, and keep it brief. Confidence comes from practice, not from having every answer memorized.
