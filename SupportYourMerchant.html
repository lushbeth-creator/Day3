How Do I Best Support My Merchant Long Term?
How do I best support my clients long term?

Let them know you are there. "Wellness" checks let the clients know you are still there for them 10 days and 10 months down the road.

What are good ways to keep in touch?

Letting them know you are there to support can be as simple as sending a text message, or you can even stop by and say hello when you're in the area.

How often?

You want to be mindful of their time and not take away from their goals of running a business, however, I would check in at least once a quarter.
