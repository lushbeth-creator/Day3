How to Spot an Opportunity

Ask Every Merchant

Their Answer Is Your Lead

"Are you using any software to manage your business?"
Their answer is your lead.
Quick Qualifier

Should You Reach Out?

"Is it used by multiple businesses?" "Do their customers need to accept payments?"
If yes to both, reach out.
