Timing, Language & Genuine Connection
The Referral Program

Referrals aren't luck. They're the result of timing, genuine connection, and knowing how to ask.

Pick the Right Time

In your QAR meeting, after you've confirmed they're satisfied. During a service call, right after you've solved a problem. During a rate review, when the value is fresh and visible.

Make It Part of the Conversation

"What are your favorite restaurants nearby?" "Who are your trusted vendors?" "Does your spouse or a family member own a business?" Every answer is a potential introduction.

Leverage Your Follow-Up

After the first statement, trust is at its highest. After a dispute is resolved in their favor, emotional goodwill peaks here. After a milestone, first year, record month, new location, celebrate then ask.

Build Genuine Connections

Know their name, busy season, their regulars. Show up without a pitch sometimes. Connect two of your merchants when there's a real fit, you become indispensable to both.
