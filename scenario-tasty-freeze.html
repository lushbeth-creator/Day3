Now Role-Play It

Class Activity · 2 Minutes

Make the Follow-Up Call

Pair up. One person plays Rachel (or Nate, if your pair decides to time it around Tuesday morning instead), the other plays the rep making the follow-up call. Act it out live, no scripts, just what you'd actually say.

"Ring, ring... 'Tasty Freeze, this is Rachel.'" Start there and let it unfold naturally from your answers to the discussion questions above.

If time allows, swap roles and run it again, once reaching Rachel, once reaching Nate. Notice how the opening changes depending on who picks up.
